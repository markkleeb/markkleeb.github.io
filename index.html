About

The Death By Audio Arcade is a series of local multiplayer arcade cabinets produced by NYC-based indie game developers at the legendary music venue, Death by Audio, in Brooklyn, NY.

Now Featuring...

DEATHMATCH BY AUDIO V! ///

The Death By Audio Arcade returns in fine form at WAKA WAKA on February 25th!

DBAA will be debuting a brand new cabinet for Jane Friedhoff's SLAM CITY ORACLES as well as featuring the return of DBA mainstays CRYSTAL BRAWL, PARTICLE MACE, POWERBOAT ITALIA '88, FUTBOL FOREVER, NEST, and NOTHING GOOD CAN COME OF THIS.

$5 - DOORS AT 6PM
TOURNAMENTS AND MUSIC AT 8PM

Upcoming Games + Events

SLAM CITY ORACLES ///

SLAM CITY ORACLES is Jane Friedhoff's dance apocalypse simulator! Part Katamari, part Slave of God, and part Rampage, SCO is about dancing so hard you tear down a city in the process.
