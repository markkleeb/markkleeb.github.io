About

The Death By Audio Arcade is a series of local multiplayer arcade cabinets produced by NYC-based indie game developers at the legendary music venue, Death by Audio, in Brooklyn, NY.

Now Featuring...

DBAA RETURNS TO MAGFEST! ///

DBAA is proud to be returning to Magfest 2016 to represent indie arcades and indie game developers!

Short for "Music And Gaming Festival," MAGFest is a four day-long event dedicated to the appreciation of video game music, gaming of all types, and the gaming community.The event runs 24 hours a day, and offers consoles, arcades, tabletop, LAN, live video game cover bands, chiptunes, vendors, guest speakers, and much much more.

Join us on Feb 18-21st!
