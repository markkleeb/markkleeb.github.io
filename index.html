About

The Death By Audio Arcade is a series of local multiplayer arcade cabinets produced by NYC-based indie game developers and exhibited at Death by Audio in Brooklyn. Currently, over 8 cabinets have been produced and have appeared at events such as the lwlvl and Maker Faire NYC.

Now Featuring...

Particle Mace ///

PARTICLE MACE is an upcoming game for Windows/Mac/iOS by Andy Wallace about swinging particles into enemies in a hostile universe. PARTICLE MACE is a game about capturing the thrill of classic arcade games with a new, physics based mechanic. PARTICLE MACE is a game about trying not to die, but that's impossible.

Powerboat Italia '88 ///

POWERBOAT ITALIA '88 is frenetic speedboat racing game that combines old-school button mashing with precision maneuvering and timing. Up to 4 powerboaters storm their way toward the prospect of supreme victory and unending fame by boosting over hurdles, snagging powerups, and blocking the paths of their rivals. It's a true test of endurance... and a measure of just how far you'll go to win.

Upcoming Games + Events

SLAM CITY ORACLES ///

SLAM CITY ORACLES is Jane Friedhoff's dance apocalypse simulator! Part Katamari, part Slave of God, and part Rampage, SCO is about dancing so hard you tear down a city in the process.
